  2. Privacy Policy

Wave Games LLC ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, including children, and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws such as COPPA, CCPA, and GDPR. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect Personal Data, including that of children under the age of 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions).

1. Who Collects and Processes Your Personal Data?

Wave Games LLC is the data controller responsible for the processing of your Personal Data within our Apps and Games. In some cases, data may also be processed by our authorized service providers acting as data processors under contractual obligations.

2. How Do We Collect Your Personal Data?

We collect Personal Data in the following ways:

  • Automatically through our Apps (device ID, OS, usage data, IP address)
  • When a user provides it voluntarily (e.g., email for support or password recovery)
  • From third-party platforms or SDKs integrated into our Apps (e.g., analytics)

We do not request or require users to provide more data than is necessary for the intended functionality.

3. How Do We Handle Children’s Personal Data?

We comply with COPPA and GDPR-K requirements when collecting data from children. Specifically:

  • We collect only minimal data necessary to operate and maintain our services (e.g., age, device data)
  • We do not collect names, exact locations, or contact details without verified parental consent
  • We do not use Personal Data of children for behavioral advertising
  • Parents/legal guardians have the right to access, review, delete, or refuse further data collection

Requests should be sent to contact@wavegames.io

4. Which Personal Data Do We Process and How?

We may process the following categories of data:

  • Device Data: device type, OS, IP address, language, screen resolution
  • Usage Data: gameplay behavior, session activity, crash logs
  • Age Information: to determine whether a user is a child
  • Optional Contact Info: email or phone number (only with verified consent)

This data is used only to:

  • Operate, secure, and improve the Apps
  • Provide customer support
  • Monitor usage and fix technical issues
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws

We do not engage in profiling or automated decision-making affecting user rights.

5. Data Retention Period

We retain Personal Data only for as long as needed to:

  • Fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When data is no longer necessary, we delete or anonymize it.

6. Automated Decision-Making or Profiling

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling in any way that produces legal or similarly significant effects on users, including children.

7. Third Parties Who May Access Your Personal Data

We work with third-party service providers strictly for:

  • App analytics (e.g., Unity Analytics, Firebase)
  • Error tracking (e.g., GameAnalytics)
  • Hosting and backend infrastructure

All providers are contractually bound to process data securely, only on our behalf, and in compliance with child protection laws.

We do not share Personal Data of children with advertisers or for any profiling or monetization purposes.

8. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under GDPR, COPPA, and other laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Know what data we process
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your or your child’s data
  • Right to restriction: Limit how your data is processed
  • Right to object: Object to specific processing activities
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@wavegames.io

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be published in our Apps and/or on our website. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For questions, requests, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of Personal Data, please contact us at:

contact@wavegames.io
